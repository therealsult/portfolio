I'm Abdullah Sultan, a passionate and committed front-end designer that has focused his career on building beautiful and intuitive websites and web applications. I have a good sense of aesthetic design and a thorough grasp of user experience principles. In order to ensure that websites appear fantastic and work flawlessly on a variety of devices and browsers, my goal is to close the gap between creativity and functionality.
